Ballroom

Tryst hall is a beaux-art treasure with a 5000-square foot circular stain-glass ceiling and seven tear-drop crystal chandeliers. The Grand Ballroom has been featured in several national bridal magazines.

Foyer

Staged with beautiful furniture, Taglyan Foyer is a spacious and elegant setting for pre-function cocktails, small receptions or even an intimate wedding ceremony. The Foyer also boosts a private smoking patio with comfortable outdoor furniture and ample lighting.

Garden

Imagine a botanical garden as the backdrop for your special event. With the sun softly filtered by nature’s best, amidst the sounds of trickling fountain water, The Tryst Hall provide an extraordinary setting for your treasured memories. The Tryst Hall are also available for photo sessions.
